My Favourite Movie: In the Mouth of Madness (1994)

Plot Summary

Infamous horror novelist Sutter Cane suddenly disappears, prompting investigator John Trent to begin his search for a manuscript with the power to alter reality as well as destroy it altogther.
Cane's editor, Linda Styles, joins Trent as he delves further into the depths of madness, drawn nearer to the threat of everlasting doom as he loses grip on the fleeting comfort of his sanity.
